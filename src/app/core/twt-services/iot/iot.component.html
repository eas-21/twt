Weighbridge Automation System

  • The proposed system entails installing RFID readers at weighbridge sites to automate data collection, making weighbridge operations independent of operators.
  • Each weighbridge will have an RFID reader to identify vehicles upon arrival, with data automatically transmitted to the central server.
  • Authenticated vehicles only can enter the Weigh bridge - boom barrier. Upon authentication, the boom barrier open sand allows the vehicle to pass through the weigh bridge.
  • Weighing information from any available weighbridge application will also be captured and interfaced with the central server via broadband connectivity.
  • The trucks entering the weighbridge captures the truck in and out time.
  • The weight of the waste collected can be synced to the software with the vehicle details.
  • Average waste collected by a vehicle in a route through the week and month are provided for analysis
  • This web-based architecture will provide real-time visualization of data.
